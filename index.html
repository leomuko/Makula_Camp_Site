About Makula Native Adventures

Inspired by our love for nature, at Makula Native, we aim to offer you a rewarding experience, whether you are looking for a space to relax and be reinvigorated or a place to focus and get work done.
Makula is a place Where leisure and productivity meet ."Get work Done as You Enjoy The OutDoor Environment.
Makula is located halfway between Entebbe and Kampala, approximately 30km from the hustle and bustle of busy city. Far enough to offer you serenity while keeping you close to. Close enough to catch your next flight without beating traffic and close enough for your next down-town physical meeting.
